# EDGL — The Rising Star
*energized, desired, genuine, lonely*


You building a real presence and reputation online, but you're still feeling lonely. Close, real connections aren't quite there yet, even though you're surrounded by increasing numbers of people. Shouldn't you know some people better by now? 

You might have found an audience rather than friends, or started to think that your platforms are better at broadcasting than creating mutual friendships. It's worth asking what it would look like to pick a smaller selection of people that you want to form some deeper relationships with and invest more deeply in those relationships, instead of spreading your attention amongst everyone equally...would that give you what you're looking for? How do you figure out which people to spend more time with?
