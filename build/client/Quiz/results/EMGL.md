# EMGL — The Ghost in the Machine
*energized, mandatory, genuine, lonely*

You show up as yourself and the content genuinely interests you—but real connection isn't materializing. Despite approaching others genuinely and with the best of intentions, something about the platforms or the way you're using them isn't translating into real relationships. It starts feeling like an obligation to remain on those platforms, but you're also questioning why you *have* to be on there when you're not making progress towards connecting with the people you want to. 

It might be worth exploring whether you're looking for connection in the right places for the relationships you want to develop——not all platforms will incentivize the types of connections that speak to you and you might have better luck on a different one. If it's not the platform, maybe it's less the machine and more about how and what you're communicating to others.
