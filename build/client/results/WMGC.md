# WMGC — The Loyal Friend
*weary, mandatory, genuine, connected*

You're on social tech because your people are there, and you want to show up for them. Because of this, you have created real connections through social platforms, where you feel like they see you and you see them...but all the digital interaction required to build and maintain these relationships drains you. You're essentially paying a tax to stay close to the people you love.

It's worth looking at whether there are lower-cost ways to maintain those relationships, or if there are ways to reduce the demands for your attention so you're not so overwhelmed.
