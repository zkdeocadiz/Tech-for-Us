# WDGL — The Hamster on the Wheel

You're putting genuine effort into building a presence and status online that you feel like you can be proud of, and it's draining you...and yet real connection still feels out of reach. You just keep running in circles, doing all the things you think you should be doing, and you're still getting frustrated because you aren't seeing any results. 

It's worth stepping back and asking whether what you're chasing is actually what you want...or just what the platform makes it seem like you *should* pursue. And if it is, the platform itself might be getting in your way, so you may want to reassess your approach.

### Words to describe your relationship with tech
- stuck in a cage
- jealousy
- dreaming of what could be

## What you need

Find your own metrics around what success looks like, because you don't feel like you're getting what you want out of these social platforms. What platforms might have incentives that match what you're really looking for? Does it look like meaningful connections?
