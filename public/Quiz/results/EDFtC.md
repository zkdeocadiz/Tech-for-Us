# EDFtC — The Community Socialite

You're the epitome of the party host of social tech—energetic, enthusiastic, and pretty great at keeping up with everything. The system feels like it's working for you and you generally benefit from social tech. But, much like a socialite, you're also aware of the consequences of bad press. You know how anything you put out on social tech might come back to haunt you in the future, and that is a constant consideration for you when you think about how you'd like to portray yourself online.

Now, that might not be a bad thing. You're protecting yourself and that's admirable... but it's worth asking if you might be missing out on amazing experiences that your participation ultimately becomes inconsequential in the future, and whether that tradeoff even matters to you in the first place.

### Words to describe your relationship with tech
- playing the field
- wary
- connection en masse

## What you need

The current systems honestly work for you—you know how to leverage them in the best ways that move you towards your goals—but it might be good to carve out some space to connect with others more deeply and more authentically.

If this is something that you feel like you want, try to make a list of the top 5 people who you want to know more and make the effort to ask more about their lives.
