# WMFpC — The Method Actor

You have real connections that you've made through social platforms, in spite of your frustrations with the platforms themselves...but maintaining these relationships is a struggle. You can't quite be yourself online, due to context collapse, platform norms, or something more personal. This has led you to feel like you need to show up in a certain way for the people you care about, which is increasingly tiring to keep up.

The question is whether there are spaces, online or off, where you can stray away from the role you feel immersed in and drop the act. What are ways that your online relationships can support you, even when you feel like you’re not at your best?

### Words to describe your relationship with tech
- putting on a good show
- ways to show you care
- being in the loop
