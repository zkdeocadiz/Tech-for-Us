# WMFtC — The Method Actor

You have real connections that you've made through social platforms, in spite of your frustrations with the platforms themselves...but maintaining these relationships costs you. You can't quite be yourself online, due to context collapse, platform norms, or something more personal——you always feel like you have to be aware of what you're disclosing in case it comes back to haunt you——and it makes you tired of having to show up in a certain way. The question is whether there are spaces, online or off, where you could drop the act.

### Words to describe your relationship with tech
- putting on a good show
- ways to show you care
- being in the loop
