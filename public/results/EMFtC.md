# EMFC — The Chameleon
*energized, mandatory, filtered (tracked), connected*

You're good at connecting with people online, regardless of whether you met them in person or digitally, and it has played a role in helping you connect with at least a small group of people that you don't feel like you would have been as close to otherwise. But you also know that the version that you share online is a limited version of yourself, because you're very aware that whatever you put out into the internet might stick around. This might be starting to feel a bit stifling and that's the main source of your unease with social media——it's starting to feel like an obligation in order to keep up relationships. You have the connections; the question is whether any of them know the real you and how much that matters to the depth of connections you want to make.
