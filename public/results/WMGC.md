# **WMGC — The Loyal Friend**

*weary, mandatory, genuine, connected*

You're on social tech because your people are there, and you show up genuinely for them — which shows in the real connections you have. But it drains you. You're essentially paying a tax to stay close to the people you love. Worth looking at whether there are lower-cost ways to maintain those relationships, or how to reduce the surrounding noise.
