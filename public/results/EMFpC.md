# EMFC — The Chameleon
*energized, mandatory, filtered (polished), connected*

You're good at connecting with people online, regardless of whether you met them in person or digitally, and it has played a role in helping you connect with at least a small group of people that you don't feel like you would have been as close to otherwise. But you do this by adapting to their expectations and that means you're constantly showing up based on what everyone else wants to see, rather than what feels most true to you. This might be starting to feel a bit stifling and that's the main source of your unease with social media——it's starting to feel like an obligation in order to keep up relationships. You have the connections; the question is whether any of them know the real you and how much that matters to the depth of connections you want to make.
